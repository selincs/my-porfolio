The Best Shows or Movies According to Selin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Adventure Time

This is my favourite show! I think it's really unique to grow alongside all of the characters in terms of age and story.

Spirited Away

My first anime movie as a child, and it changed me forever. Better or worse? Who can really say...

Moonrise Kingdom

Wes Andersen's cinematography really changed my perspective on movies and the power of perspective in general. I love this one.
